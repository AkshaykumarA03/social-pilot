## âš ï¸ Common Issues & Fixes

### Issue: "Cannot connect to backend"
**Fix**: Make sure `npm start` is running in terminal

### Issue: "Posts not saving"
**Fix**: Check that `data/` folder exists

### Issue: "Scheduled posts not publishing"
**Fix**: Keep backend running, it checks every minute

### Issue: "Strange spacing/colors"
**Fix**: Hard refresh browser (Ctrl+Shift+R)

### Issue: "Port 5000 in use"
**Fix**: Kill other process or change port in `server.js`
